* 2 7oz cans chicken
* 1 15.5oz can black beans
* 1 15.5oz can pinto beans
* 1 15.25oz can corn
* 1 small can olives
* 3 tablespoons taco seasoning
* 3/4 cup water

Mix ingredients thoroughly and reheat (5 minutes in Aroma cooker does the job). Top with shredded cheese.
